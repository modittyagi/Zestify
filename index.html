The Problem

Why every other tool
is failing you

The AI output sounds nothing like you
Every existing tool generates generic, templated content. Your audience can identify AI-written posts instantly and engagement drops accordingly. You end up spending more time editing than it would have taken to write from scratch.
02
Setup requires technical expertise you do not have
The most capable open-source tools require Docker, server configuration, and OAuth setup. That is not a reasonable expectation for a founder, consultant, or business owner who simply wants to post content consistently.
03

Analytics tell you what happened. They do not tell you why, and they do not automatically write more of what worked. Every tool stops at data. The feedback loop that drives real growth is missing entirely.
04
Agency management is an afterthought
Managing multiple client accounts across existing tools is painful, expensive, and impossible to white-label. Agencies are paying enterprise prices for functionality that should be standard.
